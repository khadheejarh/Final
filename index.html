How AwayyBus Works

Our ticket reservation process is simple and efficient

  • 1 - Reserve your ticket via App or Web

    Enter your current location & destination, choose a preffered time and date of journey and proceed to check the buses available.

  • 2 - Choose a Bus

    Check through avaialble buses, make a choice and enter your email/mobile.

  • 3 -Make Online Payment

    Make your online payment using mobile money on any network, wait a while and recieve your ticket (Seat number, Bus registration number and a Ticket verification code.)
